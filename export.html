Who we are

Grown in Brazil,
trusted worldwide.

MacaNuts is a single-origin macadamia producer located in Minas Gerais, Brazil. We control every step of production — from cultivation and harvesting to processing and packaging — ensuring consistent quality and full supply chain transparency.

We have been exporting macadamia for several years, with China as our primary international market. Our operations are registered with Brazil's RADAR export system, enabling direct international trade.

Why trust us

Credentials &
traceability.

RADAR Registration

Registered with Brazil's RADAR system (Receita Federal), enabling us to export directly without intermediaries. All shipments are fully documented.

Full traceability

Every batch is traceable to the farm, harvest season and processing date. We provide complete documentation for each shipment upon request.

Own processing

We grow, harvest, process and package on-site. No outsourced steps, no unknown supply chain. Transparent from soil to shipment.
